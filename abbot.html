Greetings! Welcome to MiGG




About MiGG

My indie Game Gallery (MiGG) is a passion project dedicated to showcasing the wonders and fun of the indie gaming space. In a world that often leans toward mainstream titles, it's easy for the incredible work of small creators to be overlooked.

This site is my personal gallery of games I love, curated to prove a simple idea: games aren't just entertainment; it's an experience. Additionally, a great game doesn't need a high budget to deliver a powerful experience. Here, you'll find creative visuals, peculiar mechanics, and, most importantly, a wide variety of emotions and experiences.

My goal is to share a fresh perspective and bring fun to fellow players, while also supporting the indie creators whose work I admire. Consider this website my love letter to their craft. This isn't a store or a review site. It's a personal gallery of games that have surprised me, moved me, or just given me a really good time. I hope this place helps you find your next favorite game and reminds you why indie games are so special.

Every game you see here is one I've played and personally vetted. This isn't a list generated by an algorithm or a paid ad. If it's on MiGG, it's because I truly believe it's special and worth your time.

A Quick Note on How I Pick Games


I handpicked every single game here based on the unique feelings they evoke and the memorable experiences they offer. My main rule is simple: if a game stuck with me long after I finished playing — whether because of its creativity, its heart, its narrative, or its sheer weirdness — then it belongs on MiGG. This isn't about scores or popularity; it's about connecting with something genuinely different.

What You'll Find Here


The games in MiGG are organized by the feelings and experiences they evoke, not just traditional genres. Each category is filled with handpicked titles that perfectly capture that specific vibe. Here's a guide to the categories you can explore:

  • RPG - Rich stories, character progression, and worlds to get lost in.
  • Puzzle - Brain-teasers and logic challenges that make you think.
  • Multiplayer - Games best enjoyed with friends (or rivals!).
  • Humorous/Funny - Experiences packed with humor, wit, and absurdity.
  • Action/Adventure - Fast-paced challenges, exploration, and thrilling gameplay.
  • Rage - Brutally challenging games that test your patience and skills.
  • Narrative/Story-Driven - Experiences where the plot and characters take center stage.
  • Meta - Games that break the fourth wall and play with reality itself.
  • Casual/Cozy - Comforting, relaxing games perfect for unwinding.
  • Rhythm - Titles where music and timing are key to the experience.
  • Horror - Classic scares, creepy atmospheres, and things that go bump in the night.
  • Disturbing - Games that tackle difficult themes and leave a lasting psychological impact.
  • ⚠️ Content Warning: These games deal with heavy subject matter.⚠️

How You Can Help


Loving these games is just the first step! If you find something you like here, the best thing you can do is buy it directly from the developer on platforms like Steam or Itch.io. Supporting them ensures they can keep making the unique games we all love.
